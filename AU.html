About Us

We are grade 9 students that come from Hanoi International School. This website was created as a required project, therefore we came together, chose rhino as our focused topic, putting many efforts and commitment to the creation of the website. The aim of this website is to raise awareness all around the world, preventing illegal poachings and rhinos from extinction. We are trying to take action, reaching out to people, asking them to spread the message, also donating to organizations, calling people to be involved in saving the endangered rhinos. We believe that if everyone comes together, this horrible issue can develop a big improvement.
